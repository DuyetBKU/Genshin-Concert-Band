THE BAND

We love music

Welcome to Chapter of the New Era, where we celebrate the talented musicians of the Genshin Impact universe! Our fictional band takes inspiration from the vibrant world of Teyvat, blending captivating melodies with dynamic storytelling. Each band member brings their unique flair and musical style, reflecting the rich diversity of Genshin’s beloved characters. From epic anthems to soulful ballads, we strive to capture the essence of adventure and camaraderie. Whether you're a devoted traveler or a music enthusiast, join us on this creative journey, where the spirit of Genshin meets the magic of music!
